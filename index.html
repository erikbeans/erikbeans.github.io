About

Erik Beans is an accomplished engineering leader with over 23 years of experience in the tech industry, holding Senior Software Engineering Manager positions at Google and C3.ai. He brings deep expertise in building scalable systems, leading high-performing teams, and driving strategic product initiatives. With an M.B.A. from UC Berkeley Haas School of Business and a B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from UC Berkeley, Erik combines technical excellence with business acumen to solve complex engineering challenges.

Beyond his professional work, Erik is actively involved in education and community leadership. He is a parent and supporter of progressive education initiatives. Erik is passionate about entrepreneurship, finance, and real estate investing, and brings these interests to bear in advisory and board roles.
