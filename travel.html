My Travel Journey

Travel is one of my greatest passions. From honeymoon adventures in Africa to exploring ancient history in Europe, each journey has enriched my life and broadened my perspective. I believe that experiencing different places, people, and cultures is essential to understanding our world and growing as individuals.

Below are some of my favorite travel experiences. Each blog captures the adventures, discoveries, and memorable moments from these journeys. Feel free to explore the detailed blog posts and photo galleries for each destination.

My Travels

🦃 Turkey

Explored the rich history and vibrant culture of Turkey, from the stunning Blue Mosque and Topkapi Palace in Istanbul to the otherworldly landscapes of Cappadocia. Highlights included a thrilling hot air balloon ride over ancient rock formations, river canoeing, and discovering the historic Grand Bazaar. The trip combined architectural wonders, local flavors, and unforgettable experiences.

❄️ Iceland

A multi-day adventure featuring biking around the Golden Circle, walking on glaciers, and chasing waterfalls. Experienced Iceland's geothermal wonders including Geysir geysers and bubbling mud pots at Lake Myvatn. Whale watching in the north, exploring dramatic fjords, and soaking in natural hot springs made this an unforgettable Nordic adventure.

🏛️ Hadrian's Wall & Scotland

A historic trek following Hadrian's Wall from Newcastle to the Scottish border, exploring Roman forts, ancient ruins, and medieval castles. The journey concluded in Scotland with visits to the Isle of Skye's dramatic landscapes, including the Old Man of Storr and the enchanting Fairy Glen. A perfect blend of history and breathtaking scenery.

🌍 East Africa Safari

An incredible honeymoon adventure through Kenya and Tanzania, featuring walking safaris in the Serengeti, wildlife encounters including lions and leopards, and exploring Zanzibar's historic Stone Town. Visited pristine islands, scuba dived in coral reefs, and enjoyed unforgettable moments in one of Africa's most beautiful regions.
